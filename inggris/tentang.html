About Us

Museum Kota Makassar merupakan instansi yang dibentuk dan diawasi dibawah dinas pariwisata dan kebudayaam kota makassar, pada 7 Juni 2000 museum ini berdiri atas ide H.B Amiruddin Maula yang saat itu sedang menjabat sebagai walikota makassar. Museum Kota Makassar ditujukan untuk membantu masyarakat dalam penyaluran informasi tentang identitas budaya dan sejarah penduduknya.

Buildings with neo-classical colonial nuances include a mixture of renaisancece and ghotic that add to the appeal of this building. This building is also the only government building in the Dutch era which was built outside the Rotterdam fort, although neo-classical style, the concept of this building applies the concept of "garden city" in question is a building surrounded by courtyards front, back, left side and right

The Makassar City Museum has 938 well-preserved collections and exhibited mainly artifacts and objects related to the development of Makassar City from the Dutch era until now, the museum has 2 floors and 1 mezzanine which has been used as an audiovisual room by the museum.

VISION
Being the best place to connect people with the History and Culture of Makassar City
MISSION

1. Improving the quality of facilities and infrastructure to support a comfortable learning
2. Increase the professionalism of museum management staff and foster in themselves the value of "Museum in My Heart"
3. The preservation of cultural heritage as a means of quality, communicative and effective learning
4. Making the Makassar City Museum a medium for learning history, art and culture
5. Establishing cooperative relations with academic institutions
