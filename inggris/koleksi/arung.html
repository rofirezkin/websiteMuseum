Arung Palakka (Sultan Of Bone)

Arung palakka was Sultan Bone who served in 1672-1696, while still a prince he led his kingdom to achieve independence from the Gowa Sultanate in 1666. Arung palakka collaborated with the Dutch Company (VOC) when it captured Makassar. Arung palakka also made the Bugis tribe as the greatest maritime power that cooperated with the Dutch and dominated the area for a century.
