Queen Wilhelmia's artifacts

One of the artifacts that is quite interesting to the public is the artifact of Queen Wilhelmia, Queen Wilhelmia was a nobleman who was born in the Netherlands in 1880 and died in 1962. He was the son of King William III and Queen Emma, but in 1884 King William died so the Queen Wilhelmia was taught to be a queen when she reached 18 years of age.
Artifacts in the form of a Queen Wilhelmia statue and also a relief of the face of Queen Wilhelmia became an award from the Netherlands in Makassar City in 1906 where Makassar was considered to be a potential area in the Eastern Archipelago.
