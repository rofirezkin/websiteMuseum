Bongaya Agreement

The Bungaya Agreement or commonly known as Bongaya was a peace agreement between the Dutch VOC represented by Admiral Cornelis Speelman and the Gowa Sultanate represented by Sultan Hasanuddin, signed on 18 November 1667, although it was stated that the peace agreement was the fact that the agreement was a declaration of the defeat of Gowa from the Dutch VOC and also a monopoly ratification by the VOC for trading a number of goods in the port of Makassar (controlled by the Sultanate of Gowa).
