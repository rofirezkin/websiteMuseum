Traditional Bugis-Makassar Wedding Dress

The traditional clothes that are still being used are Bugis - Makassar tribal traditional clothes, there is an exhibition of Bugis - Makassar tribal bridal traditional clothes on display. The traditional wedding dress with sarong and accessories among the Bugis people - Makassar is called "Kolo Dress" (traditional traditional clothing), or tutu suit (Makassar) or "Bella Dada" (Bugis) for the bridegroom and "Bodo Dress" for the bride . The colors of Bugis - Makassar traditional customs vary greatly among others: red, green, orange, blue, and others.
