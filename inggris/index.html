Welcome to Makassar City Museum

Makassar City Museum is an agency that was formed and overseen under the Makassar tourism and cultural office, on 7 June 2000 the museum stood on the idea of H.B Amiruddin Maula who was then serving as mayor of Makassar. Makassar City Museum is intended to assist the public in the distribution of information about the cultural identity and history of its population.
